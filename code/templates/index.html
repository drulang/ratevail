Vail started as someone's idealistic vision,

driven by a single passion, and went on to become the largest ski resort in Colorado and the United States, and a destination for vacationers all over the world. Vail mountain and the subsequent towns of Vail, East and West Vail, and Lionshead were all built with the sole purpose of offering a once and a lifetime experience, and it does just that.
